About My Project


My project is focused on a taco restaurant that offers a variety of delicious and authentic Mexican cuisine inspired by mediaeval times. The menu will feature a wide range of tacos, including classics such as carne asada, al pastor, and carnitas, as well as unique and innovative options like pineapple swords and shrimp spears. The website for this restaurant is designed for people who want a unique food experience with a variety of spices and seasonings, all without the hassle. It allows customers to easily order food for delivery right to their doorstep. I chose to create a taco restaurant website as my project because I wanted to try something new after previously creating a website focused on a car dealership with a database of car parts.
